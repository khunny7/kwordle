K-Wordle Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-27

Overview

K-Wordle is a simple word game built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers.

What data we process

  • Local storage: Your game history and preferences (e.g., chosen word length) are stored only on your device via your browser's local storage.
  • Service worker cache: For offline capability, static files may be cached on your device.

No names, email addresses, payment info, or other personal data are collected.

Third-party services

The app is hosted on Firebase Hosting. We do not use third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs within the app.

Children’s privacy

The app does not target children and does not collect personal information from users of any age.

Data retention and control

You can clear your browser data to remove game history and cached files at any time. Uninstalling the PWA will also remove locally stored data associated with the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For questions or requests, please open an issue at github.com/khunny7/kwordle/issues.
